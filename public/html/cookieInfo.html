What is a cookie?

Cookies are small text files that your browser uses to store information. Read more

Why does this site use cookies?

This site uses cookies mainly to store your username so you won't have to enter it every time you visit this site. When you give your consent to the use of cookies, a cookie will be created to remember your consent so you won't have to be prompted every time you visit this site.

Can I disable the use of cookies?

Yes! You can disable the use of cookies in your browser. For more information about how to disable cookies, please read the help material of your browser or device. More about managing cookies
